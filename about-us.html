About Us

Get to know the team that is working hard to bring new and exciting functionality to the off-plan PropTech industry.

At Jestalt we create experiences, we tell stories, we paint the future to come, we help people to visualize their ideas and turn simple lines on paper into vivid and immersive personal experiences which capture the imagination. Our work leaves lasting impressions and takes you on a journey to where ever you want to go. The future at your finger tips. Our company’s speciality is property, and how to market it.

We create experiences for your buyers to showcase your unbuilt properties in various ways, using various technologies, all in the aid of encouraging buyers to feel confident enough to make the final purchase. We tailor make solutions for each business, as no ”one size fits all” approach works when it comes to the successful marketing of any high end property. Our solutions range from interactive virtual experiences for large corporate real estate enterprises, to bespoke architectural films for special one off properties.

Elevator PITCH

Jestalt is currently in a state of change. We are using our skills and backgrounds in architecture, visualization, and the property sales market, to develop interactive tools to revitalise the off-plan property market.

We are looking for property related industry professionals to connect with. Our goals are to discuss our platform in conjunction with your business, sharing knowledge of our experiences from within different parts of the industry, and talk broadly on where we believe it is headed.

Through these discussions we hope to develop a keen perspective on the property market in both South Africa & the United Kingdom. To assess what opportunities may lie within the sector, and what key opportunities our new platform can look to provide solutions for.

We are also looking for the right investment partners. If you are interested in meeting with us to discuss our solutions for the PropTech space please get in touch.
